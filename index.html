02

How do my clients book a mini session?

Your clients can easily book a mini session on Niceshoots by simply selecting a time slot and entering their details. It's that quick and easy!

03

Can I require a deposit/retainer for secure bookings?

Yes! You can set a deposit/retainer, and your clients will pay it right when they book.

04

Can I offer add-ons during the booking process?

Yes, you can easily offer add-ons during the booking process for clients to choose from. It’s a great way to boost your earnings!

05

How do online contracts and questionnaires work?

You can set up online contracts and questionnaires, so your clients can seamlessly sign contracts and fill out questionnaires during the booking process.

06

Is the booking page mobile-friendly?

Yes, the booking page is fully mobile-friendly, and it works seamlessly on tablets and desktops too, making it easy for clients to book from any device.

07

Can I customize my booking page to fit my brand?

Yes, you can customize your booking page to match your brand with your colors, logo, and your own domain.

08

How does the waitlist feature work?

When you enable the waitlist feature, if your mini session is fully booked, clients can join the waitlist. Niceshoots will notify them whenever a time slot becomes available.
